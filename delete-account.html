Delete Your Unwyned Account

Request Account Deletion

To delete your Unwyned account and all associated data, send an email to:

privacy@unwyned.com

Subject line: "Delete My Unwyned Account"

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to privacy@unwyned.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete My Unwyned Account"
  3. Include the email address associated with your Unwyned account
  4. Confirm that you want your account permanently deleted

We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

Data Type Action
Your taste profile and quiz answers Deleted
Saved wines in your Stash Deleted
Wine ratings and history Deleted
Achievement and badge progress Deleted
Account email and display name Deleted
App preferences and settings Deleted

What We May Retain

Some data may be retained for legal and business purposes:

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Purchase/subscription records Up to 7 years Tax and financial compliance
Anonymized analytics Indefinite Not linked to your identity
Legal correspondence As required Legal compliance

Before You Delete

  • This action is permanent. Your data cannot be recovered after deletion.
  • If you have an active subscription, cancel it first through the App Store or Google Play.
  • Consider exporting your wine ratings if you want to keep a record.

Alternative Options

Not ready to delete? You have other choices:

  • Sign out: Simply sign out of the app. Your data remains but you won't be actively using it.
  • Data export: Request a copy of your data before deletion by emailing privacy@unwyned.com.
  • Reset profile: Want a fresh start? Email us to reset your taste profile while keeping your account.

Questions?

If you have questions about account deletion or your data, contact us:

  • Privacy inquiries: privacy@unwyned.com
  • General support: support@unwyned.com

For more information about how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.
